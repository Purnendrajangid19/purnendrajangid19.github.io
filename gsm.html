> GSM Signal Analysis & SDR Capture

Independent research on real-world GSM networks using RTL-SDR Blog V4 and open-source tooling such as GR-GSM, Gqrx and Wireshark. All captures are performed in a lab / controlled environment with subscriber identifiers fully redacted.

Broadcast Channel (BCCH) Mapping
Tools: RTL-SDR V4, Gqrx, GR-GSM
Captured downlink spectrum and identified active ARFCNs. Decoded BCCH bursts to enumerate cell IDs, LACs and broadcast parameters, focusing on ciphering indicators, network feature flags and handover configurations.

Encryption Posture Review
Analysed system information messages to determine whether the operator permits unencrypted traffic (A5/0) or weak ciphers. Documented where legacy configurations are still allowed, increasing risk to subscribers.
